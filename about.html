PRATIX FINANCE was founded in 2024 by Raghav Pratap, a passionate developer and financial enthusiast who recognized the need for accurate, accessible financial planning tools in India. Having personally experienced the challenges of financial planning and the complexity of available tools, Raghav set out to create a platform that would democratize financial calculations.

What started as a simple EMI calculator has evolved into India's most comprehensive financial calculator suite, trusted by over 50,000 users for making informed financial decisions.
