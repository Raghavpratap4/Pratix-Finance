Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are your calculators?

Our calculators use industry-standard formulas and are regularly updated to ensure 99.9% accuracy. However, results should be used for estimation purposes only.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes! All calculations happen locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your financial information.

Do you charge for using the calculators?

No, all our basic calculators are completely free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future, but core functionality will always remain free.

Can I use these calculators for commercial purposes?

Yes, you can use our calculators for both personal and commercial purposes. Please refer to our Terms of Service for detailed usage rights.

How often do you update the calculators?

We regularly update our calculators to reflect changes in tax laws, interest rates, and financial regulations. Updates are deployed automatically.

Can I request a new calculator?

Absolutely! We love user feedback and feature requests. Use the contact form above or email us your suggestions.
