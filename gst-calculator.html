🧾 What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based tax that is levied on every value addition. It has replaced many indirect taxes in India like VAT, service tax, and excise duty.

📈 Benefits of GST

  • ✅ Unified tax system across the country
  • 📦 Eliminates cascading tax effect
  • 📊 Higher efficiency in logistics
  • 🧾 Transparent and simple tax structure
  • 🛒 Reduced overall tax burden on consumers

📊 Why Use a GST Calculator?

A GST calculator helps you determine the GST amount payable or receivable on a given product/service. It's useful for businesses, freelancers, shopkeepers, and customers to understand accurate pricing.
