🧾 What is Income Tax?

Income tax is a direct tax imposed on an individual's or entity’s income. It helps the government fund essential services such as healthcare, education, defense, and infrastructure.

⚖️ Old vs New Tax Regime

Old Regime: Higher tax rates but allows deductions like 80C, HRA, LTA, etc.

New Regime: Lower tax rates, but without exemptions/deductions. Simple and suitable for those with fewer investments.
