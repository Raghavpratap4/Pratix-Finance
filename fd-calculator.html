📘 What is a Fixed Deposit (FD)?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a secure investment option offered by banks and financial institutions. You deposit a lump sum amount for a fixed period, and in return, you earn a guaranteed interest rate.

🌟 Key Features of FD:

  • 🔐 Safe & Risk-Free: FD offers guaranteed returns and capital protection.
  • 🗖️ Flexible Tenure: Choose durations from a few months to several years.
  • 💰 Compound Interest: Earn more when interest is compounded quarterly or monthly.
  • 🏦 Available at Banks & NBFCs: Offered by almost all major financial institutions.
  • 📤 Premature Withdrawal: Allowed with a small penalty in most cases.

🧾 Why Use an FD Calculator?

An FD calculator helps you estimate your maturity amount and total interest earned. It’s useful for planning savings, budgeting, and comparing interest rates across banks.
