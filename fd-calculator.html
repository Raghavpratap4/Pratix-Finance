FD Laddering Strategy

What is FD Laddering?

FD Laddering is an investment strategy where you split your investment across multiple FDs with different maturity periods. This provides regular liquidity and helps you take advantage of changing interest rates.

💡 Financial Tips

🎯 Diversification

Don't put all your money in FDs. Diversify across equity, debt, and real estate for better returns.

📅 Emergency Fund

Keep 6-12 months of expenses in liquid instruments like savings account or liquid funds.

💰 Tax Planning

Use tax-saving instruments like ELSS, PPF, and Tax Saver FDs to reduce your tax liability.

📈 Long-term Investing

For goals beyond 5 years, consider equity mutual funds for better inflation-adjusted returns.
