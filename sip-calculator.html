🧾 What is SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. It’s a smart and disciplined way to invest and helps in building wealth over the long term.

📈 Benefits of SIP:

  • 💸 Start with small amounts
  • 📆 Develop a regular investment habit
  • 📉 Helps balance market ups and downs (Rupee Cost Averaging)
  • ⏳ Benefit from compounding in the long run
  • 🧘‍♂️ Peace of mind through automated investments

📊 Why Use a SIP Calculator?

A SIP calculator helps you estimate how much wealth you can build through regular monthly investments. It is useful for planning major financial goals like education, retirement, buying a home, etc.
