About Pratix Finance

Pratix Finance is a modern financial web application offering intuitive and advanced calculators like SIP and EMI to help users make informed decisions. Built with simplicity and mobile-friendliness in mind, the platform is ideal for individuals seeking quick and accurate financial insights.

This app is developed and maintained by Raghav Pratap.
